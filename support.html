Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import my WhatsApp chats?

Open WhatsApp, select a chat, tap the three dots menu (⋮), choose "More" → "Export chat" → "Without Media", then share it with the Bonobo app.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes! Your chats are securely encrypted and stored in our cloud database. We never share your conversations with third parties for advertising or marketing. You can delete your data anytime.

What insights can I unlock?

Bonobo provides red/green flag analysis, attachment style assessment, compatibility scores, communication patterns, reciprocity insights, and personalized relationship tips.

How much does it cost?

Bonobo offers a free tier with basic insights including unlimited chat imports, basic compatibility test insights, and message patterns. Unlock deeper AI-powered insights with a one-time purchase or flexible subscription.

Can I use Bonobo for multiple relationships?

Yes! You can import unlimited chats and analyze as many relationships as you'd like.

How do I delete my account or data?

Contact us using the form below or email us directly, and we'll promptly remove all your data from our systems.

Which platforms are supported?

Bonobo is available on both iOS (iPhone/iPad) and Android devices. Currently, we support WhatsApp chat imports.
