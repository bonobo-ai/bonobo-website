Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Bonobo. We understand that your conversations are deeply personal, and we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered dating insights app.

Information We Collect

Chat Data

When you import a WhatsApp chat into Bonobo, we collect and store the following:

  • Message content and timestamps
  • Participant names (as they appear in the chat)
  • Message metadata (response times, message frequency)

Account Information

We collect basic account information including:

  • Email address (for account creation and communication)
  • Device information (for app functionality)
  • Purchase history (for subscription and payment management)

Analytics Data

We use analytics services (PostHog) to understand how users interact with our app:

  • App usage patterns
  • Feature engagement
  • Technical performance data

How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • AI Analysis: To generate relationship insights, compatibility scores, and personalized recommendations
  • App Functionality: To store your chats securely and provide access across your devices
  • Service Improvement: To enhance our AI models and improve the accuracy of insights
  • Customer Support: To respond to your questions and resolve technical issues

Data Storage and Security

Secure Cloud Storage: Your imported chats are encrypted and stored securely in our Supabase cloud database. This allows you to access your insights across all your devices.

Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (using HTTPS) and at rest in our database.

Access Controls: Only authorized personnel with legitimate business needs can access encrypted data, and all access is logged and monitored.

Data Sharing

We never share your chat content or personal conversations with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services We Use:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication (data storage)
  • Google Gemini AI: AI analysis engine (processes chat content to generate insights)
  • Stripe: Payment processing (handles subscriptions and purchases)
  • PostHog: Analytics (anonymized usage data only)

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and security standards. We only share the minimum data necessary for each service to function.

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete Chats: You can delete individual chats or all your chats at any time from within the app
  • Delete Account: You can request full account deletion by using our contact form
  • Export Data: Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of analytics tracking in app settings

Data Retention

We retain your chat data and insights as long as your account is active. When you delete a chat or your account, we permanently remove that data from our systems within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Bonobo is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through the app or via email notification.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please use our contact form.
